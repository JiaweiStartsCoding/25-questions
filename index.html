25 Questions

  1. How would you define being an artist? How does it make any difference from other occupation, or if it does?
  2. If it is a life-long practice, is there such an idea of success?
  3. How to apart self from a larger scale of others? It is not about compromise yourself for commercial benefits, but more about perspectives. Does your own interpretation occupy your whole world and become the only source of truth, innocence, grievance?
  4. How to organize your time and deal with procrastination？
  5. How do you know where to say a completion of a work? Is there ever a thing of completion which is either based on your satisfaction or other criteria?
  6. Is it necessary to accomplish a personal style which either distinguishes from others or grouped with others? If there is ever a moment you feel to be one of a kind, can you describe your feeling, attention or what do you think make you reach such status?
  7. How to always reflect on who I am, who I want to be while facing everyday's struggle which probably will distract us from the long-term goal?
  8. Would the "goal" change with the unexpected we experienced? is there a moment you would regret it and hope to get back to certain status?
  9. Is the regretness built upon your unability to fix the current problem?
  10. Who is your role model or fantasy? How do you make yourself like it?
  11. How to balance the stress from aspiration and enjoy every living moment?
  12. How to effectively use little fragmented time？
  13. How to develop elasticity of mind, be more open minded, inclusive, risk taking?
  14. How to find meanings in the most mundane, boring, or terrible things?
  15. How to ensure enough exercise in intensive days?
  16. How to accept your own defects or even better make it something valuable?

Feel free to add more questions if you'd like.
